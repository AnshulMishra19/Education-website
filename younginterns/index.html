Frequently Asked Questions

How does this program works?

After registration, you will undergo a focused 15-day micro-training in your chosen category. Following this, dive into an internship with India's top brands for practical experience.

How much daily time do I have to commit for training ?

Join our 30-days micro-training with live lectures of 60 mintutes. The schedule will be shared with registered students before training begins. Dive into an intensive learning experience!

How to find suitable internships on your platform?

: Students can browse through our diverse internship listings and apply directly to the ones aligning with their interests

Who is eligible to apply for internships on your platform?

Our platform welcomes school students and teenagers looking to gain valuable internship experiences

What industries do you offer internships in?

Our platform covers a wide range of industries, including technology, business, creative arts, and more.

Do you provide any training during the internship?

Yes, we offer resources and support to help interns excel, including mentorship programs and educational materials.

Are these internships remote or location-specific?

Internship locations vary; some are in-person, while others offer remote opportunities, providing flexibility for students.
